## THE HOME DEPOT

### Scale & financials
- **FY2025 net sales $164.7B** (precisely $164,683M), up 3.2% / ~$5.2B YoY. [IR, Feb 24 2026]
- **2,359 retail stores + 1,250+ SRS locations**, **470,000+ associates** (period ended Feb 1, 2026). SRS footprint reflects the SRS Distribution acquisition. [IR, Feb 24 2026]

### Strategy — "One Home Depot" / interconnected retail
- Multi-year **interconnected-retail strategy ("One Home Depot")** blending physical + digital for DIY and Pro; launched Dec 2017 (~$11B program), still the framing for current investment. [corporate.homedepot.com newsroom]
- CEO frames THD as a **"project retailer"** whose customers engage across many touch points (online, in aisles, increasingly AI), investing to **"remove friction and make home improvement seamless."** [IR, Mar 31 2026]

### Pro business
- **Free Pro digital workspace** for all Pro Xtra loyalty members (web + mobile app): manage projects, materials, and business across the full project lifecycle. [IR, Mar 18 2026]
- **Material List Builder AI** — integrates with Project Planning, interprets project intent, generates material lists in seconds to speed Pro bidding. [IR, Mar 18 2026]
- Positioned as **"ongoing investment to grow its ecosystem of capabilities for Pros"** toward a **"frictionless, interconnected experience"** in store or online. [IR, Mar 18 2026]

## STRIPE

### Enterprise product suite (all on stripe.com/enterprise)
Payments (online), **Terminal** (in-person), **Connect** (platforms), **Issuing** (cards), **Billing** (recurring/invoicing), **Radar** (fraud), **Tax**, **Financial Connections**, **Data Pipeline**, **Authorization Boost** (acceptance optimization), **Link** (accelerated checkout).

### Unified / omnichannel capabilities
- **Terminal unifies in-person + online**: "Manage online and in-person payments in one place for simplified reporting and a unified customer view." Works as one stack with Payments + Connect via a single onboarding flow. [stripe.com/terminal, docs]
- **Connect + embedded finance** (Issuing spending cards, Capital loans, Treasury) — relevant to **B2B / Pro contractor financial services**. Stripe cites Pro-facing field-service customers (Housecall Pro, Tekion) using these. (Pro/contractor tie is defensible inference, not verbatim.)
- B2B unification handled via **Billing/Invoicing** (separate from Terminal).

### Scale & credibility (Stripe self-reported)
- **$1.9T total volume** (2025, +34% YoY; up from $1.4T in 2024)
- **99.999% historical uptime** (cited inconsistently 99.999 vs 99.9999 — attribute to Stripe)
- **250M+ API requests/day**
- **50+ category leaders** each processing $1B+ annual volume

### The killer reference — URBN (directly analogous)
- **URBN** (Urban Outfitters, Anthropologie, Free People, FP Movement, Nuuly) **consolidated $5B of online + in-store revenue onto Stripe.**
- Migrated from multiple providers to one Stripe platform across **600–700+ stores** (Terminal, BBPOS WisePOS E readers); universal payment token, cross-channel refunds/returns/BOPIS/BOSS; ecommerce >50% of global revenue. [stripe.com/customers/urbn]
- THD-scale omnichannel proof point. (Note: Stripe says "revenue," not "volume.")
